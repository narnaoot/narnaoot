About

After twenty years in tech, most recently as a Business Process Engineer at Apple, I finished an MS in Business Analytics from Saint Mary's College. My practicum project mapped and analyzed internet access across the US.

I like using data to solve problems and guide strategy, and I'm currently looking for work. Drop me a line if you have any cool projects you'd like to collaborate on!

Fun fact: I'm a passionate reader, and occasional writer, of science fiction — particularly the stuff that explores radically different ways people can live.
