The Questions I Always Ask

my diagnostic toolkit
Phase 1 — Opening
01
It's six months from now. You are really happy. What has changed?
This is where we start. The answer tells me everything about what actually matters.
02
What is keeping you from getting your work done?
Not what you want to build — what's in the way right now.
03
What terrible, manual task do you absolutely hate doing?
This is where automation and process redesign live.
↓  then I go deeper  ↓
Phase 2 — Diagnosis
04
What's getting in your way?
Not the work itself — the friction around it. Dependencies, approvals, the things that shouldn't be hard but are.
05
What do you need to know to fix that?
I want to understand the information gap, not just the symptom. What data, clarity, or decision is missing?
06
What does the team already know that executives need to be convinced of?
Practitioners see the problem clearly. The challenge is making it legible upward — and that's a translation job.
